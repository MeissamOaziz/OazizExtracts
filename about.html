Our Story

The Refiner's Spirit

Oaziz Extracts Inc. ("Oaziz") is a privately held cannabis refiner based in the vibrant Ahuntsic district of Montreal. Specializing in traditional and modern solvent-free refining methods, we are masters of techniques like water extraction, sieving, and heat pressing. Since receiving our license from Health Canada in 2022, we have dedicated ourselves to the craft of purification.

Our journey was made possible through the belief and support of friends and family who financed our vision. This foundation makes us more than just a company; it makes us a family, immensely proud and grateful to work daily with our shared passion for cannabis. We take immense pride in having built a reputation for trust and reliability with our customers and suppliers alike.

Our Team

The management of Oaziz consists of a diverse group of individuals with deep experience in branding, product formulation, quality assurance, and financing. Above all, we are united by a fundamental knowledge and expertise in the art and science of cannabis.
